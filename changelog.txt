Version: 0.3.0
Date: 05.01.2024
  Major Features:
    - Now the audio playback is completely synchronized!
    - New entity “sound emitter” (base entity for the next part of the mod)

    - Fade in/fade out/doppler shift/max sounds per type are now hardcoded
  Debug:
